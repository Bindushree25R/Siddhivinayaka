• Shri Dhakshinabhimukha Angaaraka Mahaganapathi Temple

About the Temple

Shri Dakshinabhimukha Angaraka Mahaganapathi Temple is the first to be worshipped before beginning any new project or venture as he is the destroyer of obstacles (Vighnaharta). This is Shree Siddhivinayak Ganapati Temple at Prabhadevi in Mathikere Banglore, that fulfills the desires of the worshipers.

The temple of Lord Dakshinabhimukha Angaaraka mahaganapathi was established in the year 2013 in SBM Colony with overwhelming support and contributions from all the residents, devotees and well-wishers.

The temple was further expanded in the year 2015 with the installation of sri kanteshwara swamy, sri prassanna parvathy, sri Subramanya swamy and sri veeranjaneya swamy deites. Daily and monthly pujas and sewas are being conducted regularly along with Homas on special occasions-Rudra Homa, Sahasra Modaka Ganapathi Homa, Durga Homa, Dhanavanthri Homa, Girija kalyana etc.
